# Purchase Equipment and Gear

## Starting Credits
- A standard starting character begins with **3,000 credits**, plus any additional credits or free gear granted by their Archetype/Path.
- The GM may adjust starting wealth up or down depending on the desired power level of the campaign.

## Basic Equipment

### Pocket Comm Unit
- Effects: Connects to a quantum network or directly to other comm units; can form small squad networks.
- Cost: 50–1,200 credits (features and style vary).

### Magitech Comm Unit
- Effects: Same as Pocket Comm, plus can **receive magical missives**.
- Cost: 250–2,200 credits depending on quality and features.

## Potions & Consumables
Most potions are brewed by Drifters and are usually alcoholic. Over‑consumption can cause the **Drunk** condition.

### Mana Beer
- Effect: Instantly restore **3 Spell Pool**.
- Overuse: If more than **3** are consumed in an hour, the drinker gains the **Drunk** condition.
- Cost: 75 credits

### Healing Beer
- Effect: Instantly restore **10 hit points**.
- Overuse: If more than **3** are consumed in an hour, the drinker gains the **Drunk** condition.
- Cost: 100 credits

### Counterspell Potion
- Effect: Allows the drinker to roll **10 dice** to attempt to counter a single spell.
- Cost: 150 credits

### Greater Counterspell Potion
- Effect: Provides **5 automatic successes** to counter a target spell.
- Cost: 500 credits

### Greater Healing Beer
- Effect: Instantly restore **30 hit points**.
- Overuse: If more than **2** are consumed in an hour, the drinker gains the **Drunk** condition.
- Cost: 400 credits

### Greater Mana Beer
- Effect: Instantly restore **15 Spell Pool**.
- Overuse: If more than **2** are consumed in an hour, the drinker gains the **Drunk** condition.
- Cost: 1,000 credits
